    I 'm not going to insult your intelligence blathering on about how pretty my pages look. You can find that out for yourself. I'd rather tell you about my philosophy of designing for the Web. It's a challenging medium, and the designer doesn't have full control over the end product. Therefore, a skilled Web designer must take into account
  • the appearance of the page in suboptimal situations (old browser, no images, strange color palette, etc.),
  • the strain on the viewer's resources (disk space, memory, processor speed, etc.),
  • the fact that the person viewing the page should be able to count on a page loading in 15 seconds or less.

When I design your website, you can rest assured that these have all been taken care of. You know your site will load quickly and look good in any browser.
